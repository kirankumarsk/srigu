Campus Life & Overall Development

At the heart of our mission is a holistic commitment to nurturing every aspect of a child's physical, psychological, emotional, and intellectual development.

Our diverse curriculum and innovative teaching methods are designed to inspire each child to thrive according to their individual strengths and interests.

Please note that mobile phones are prohibited on campus.

Why Gurukula International Residential School?

Residential schools offer a unique environment that fosters holistic development beyond academics. By immersing students in a supportive community round-the-clock, these schools provide opportunities for personal growth, independence, and social skills development. Through a structured routine and dedicated faculty, students learn valuable life skills and build lifelong friendships in a safe and nurturing environment. Additionally, the residential setting allows for deeper engagement in extracurricular activities, fostering a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in both academic and personal pursuits.

Life at Gurukula International Residential School

Students thrive in a dynamic environment where they're encouraged to explore diverse interests. From basketball games to creative pursuits like art and pottery, our curriculum embraces multifaceted growth. Whether trekking through nearby hills or leading community initiatives, students actively engage with their surroundings. Additionally, we promote practices like Yoga and Pranayama to foster holistic well-being, ensuring a balanced and energized experience for all.

Classes and activities are designed to foster open interaction between teachers and students, nurturing curiosity and exploration.

Growing Together

Amidst the tranquil embrace of our campus, a vibrant sense of community flourishes. We hold firm to the belief that nurturing empathy and fostering teamwork within our microcosm ultimately shapes a broader society built upon solidarity and active citizenship.

What’s Unique

At Gurukula, the journey of childhood unfolds amidst a nurturing haven enveloped by nature's embrace. Here, we prioritize holistic growth, providing each learner with tailored attention and fostering an environment where every child's unique potential is cherished. With a proud teacher-student ratio of 1:8 and intimate class sizes of 25 to 30 students, we ensure personalized care and support, nurturing each child's development with unwavering dedication.

Fostering Integrity

Our mission is to nurture individuals who exude confidence in their innate abilities and talents. Within the Gurukula community, we foster a culture of self-awareness and integrity, where every student is empowered to flourish and embrace their authentic selves.

Growing up with bonds

Within our vibrant community, children immerse themselves in shared experiences and core values, forging enduring interpersonal bonds that transcend time. Even after graduation, our alumni network serves as a steadfast anchor, sustaining these cherished connections as students embark on their individual journeys.

Being in Touch

At GIRS, we foster the art of communication by encouraging students to write letters to their parents weekly. Through a dedicated phone system, students can connect with their families at scheduled times, while parents can reciprocate with letters or emails to both their children and the school staff. Additionally, our campus is seamlessly connected through a cutting-edge speaker system, ensuring that students and staff can be reached anywhere, creating a supportive and connected community.

Health & Wellness

Emotional Wellbeing is a vital ingredient for balanced growth and progress. Resident teachers have their living quarters adjacent to the dormitories and double up as dorm parents. They are accessible to the students at all times. Students have a tight schedule, which does not normally allow for homesickness to overcome them; but when it does happen, they are counseled and supported by dorm parents and teachers. Students are well cared for, and our teachers ensure they deal with problems effectively, helping them to adapt to their environment.

Physical Wellbeing: For any medical needs or emergencies, we have a well-equipped and high quality infirmary with trained nurses and doctors visiting the campus. All such needs are addressed and attended to immediately.

Safety

Being fully aware of the trust placed in us, safety and security of students are of paramount importance to us. We have rigorous measures in place to safeguard the campus and community. Our safety and security measures include:
– A secure and gated campus
– CCTV monitoring throughout the campus
– Trained security personnel
– Rigorous security checks at entry
– Internet safety protocols

Food

Recognizing that we are what we eat, all meals are freshly prepared on campus multiple times a day from produce sourced locally or grown in our kitchen gardens. Children also seasonally treat themselves to freshly plucked fruit from the many campus trees!

The essence of who we are is reflected in the warm, earthy colors of Amber, a sturdy yet luminous fossil gem, as unique in hue as it is in origin. Like the feeling of “captured sunlight” that Amber evokes, the unique guiding light in each child is nurtured for all learners to succeed in their unique ways.
