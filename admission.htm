Why join Sri Gurukula International?

  • At Gurukula Internaltional School: The journey of childhood unfolds amidst a nurturing haven enveloped by nature's embrace.
  • Growing up with bonds: Even after graduation, our alumni network serves as a steadfast anchor, sustaining these cherished connections as students embark on their individual journeys.
  • World class architecture for beyond classroom learning: Students are enveloped in a sense of unity with nature, as they observe the ever-changing seasons, lush foliage, and vibrant birdlife. Utilizing local stone, traditional roofing, and open verandahs, our architecture blends regional charm with modern aesthetics, creating a timeless and inviting atmosphere.
  • Success Coaching and Mentoring: Gurukula's tailored program nurtures inquisitive minds and cultivates independent thinkers—individuals who thrive on curiosity and engage with the world through exploration and discovery. We celebrate the journey of questioning and learning, igniting a lifelong passion for exploration and innovation.
  • Student led events for real learning experiences: All massive events organised by the school, Each annual event will bring each and every student to contribuite their skills and expertise and showcase their ability, and learn new things which are helpfull for day-to-day life.
